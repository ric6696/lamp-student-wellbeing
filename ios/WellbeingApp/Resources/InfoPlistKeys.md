- HealthKit
- Background Modes:

  - Background fetch (optional but recommended for periodic flush)

- NSHealthShareUsageDescription
- NSLocationWhenInUseUsageDescription
- NSLocationAlwaysAndWhenInUseUsageDescription
- NSLocationAlwaysUsageDescription

- NSMicrophoneUsageDescription (used for ambient decibel levels)
